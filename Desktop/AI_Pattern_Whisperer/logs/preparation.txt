## 🧠 Most Repeated & High-Yield Questions (Practice These)

### ✳️ Aptitude

* Time and Work → "A can do a job in 10 days..."
* Speed & Distance → "Train crosses platform..."
* Percentages → "What is x% of y?"
* Profit & Loss → "SP, CP problems"

### ✳️ Reasoning

* Number Series: Find the missing number
* Direction Problems: "A walks north then east..."
* Coding-Decoding: “If CAT = DBU…”
* Puzzle: Seating in circular or linear arrangement

### ✳️ Verbal

* Spot the error: “He go to school yesterday.”
* Fill in the blank: “Although she was tired, \_\_\_.”
* RC: Look for keywords, don’t overthink
